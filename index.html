FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website or app?+

Timelines depend on scope and complexity. A simple landing page takes 1–2 weeks. A full web application with backend typically takes 6–12 weeks. A mobile app ranges from 8–16 weeks. We always give you a detailed timeline before we begin.

Do you work with startups or only big companies?+

Both! We love working with early-stage startups building their first product as much as we enjoy helping established enterprises modernise. Our pricing is flexible to suit different budgets and stages.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?+

Absolutely. Every product we build is mobile-first and fully responsive. We test across multiple devices and screen sizes before delivery. With over 65% of Nigerian users on mobile, this is non-negotiable for us.

What happens after the project is launched?+

We don't disappear after launch. All projects include a post-launch support period (1–6 months depending on your plan). We also offer ongoing monthly maintenance and retainer packages for continuous development.

Do I own the code and designs when the project is done?+

Yes — 100%. Upon final payment, all source code, design files, and assets are transferred to you. You own everything. No lock-in, no ongoing licence fees.

How do I get started?+

Simply fill out the contact form below or send us an email. We'll schedule a free 30-minute discovery call, understand your project, and send you a detailed proposal within 48 hours.
